• Devotees are hereby informed that make donations in the Donation Office of Sri Sri Sri Pyditallamma trust in temple premises at Vizianagaram and obtain official receipt. Donations can also be done online through the official website https://online.pyditallamma.in, and obtain online receipt. These donations will reach Sri Sri Sri Pyditallamma Trust, Vizianagaram.
