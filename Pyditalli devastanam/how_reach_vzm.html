How to Reach Vizianagaram

Road

Vizianagaram bus stand located at Vizianagaram Town and is very near to Vizianagaram Railway Station. Vizianagarambus stand is nearly 55km away from visakhapatnam railway station and bus stand. You can have non stop Bus service up to night 10 PM from visakahaptanam to vizianagaram for every 20 minutes. Vizianagaram is well connected with road ways click here for location of Pyditalli Ammavari temple, Vizianagaram.

Rail

Vizianagaram railway station is located at Vizianagaram town and is very near to Vizianagaram bus stand. Vizianagaram railway station is important junction in Eco railway and southern railway zones and many trains stop here. you can travel from any big city in India to Vizianagaram in train or you can travel to Visakhapatnam and there is non stop bus service from Viskhapatnam to Vizianagaram for every 20 minutes.

Air

The nearest major airport to Vizianagaram is Visakhapatnam International Airport (also known as Vizag Airport). Visakhapatnam Airport is approximately 65 kilometers away from the vizianagaram. You can book a flight to Visakhapatnam from any major city in India and then take a taxi or a bus to Vizianagaram.
