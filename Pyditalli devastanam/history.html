History

Pyditalli ammavari temple

The presiding diety in the pyditallamma temple is pyditallamma. This is the ancient temple present in Vizianagram, Andhra Pradesh.

Goddess pyditallamma is belonged to Gajapati Dynasty and she was a sister of Late king Vijaya Rama Raju of vizianagaram. In 1756 vizianagaram faced continous battles with bobbili. Then the French army general Bussy helped Vijaya Rama Raju to attack bobbili on 23rd january 1757.

In this battle the Bobbili fort was almost demolished and many bobbili soldiers and normal people present in the fort were dead in battle. The Rama Raju's wife and pydimamba (kings sister) tried to convince the king Vijaya Rama Raju to stay away from war. At that time Pydiimamba was conducting pooja and she came to know that his brother is killed by Tandra Paparayudu in the battle. By knowing this fact pydimamba faints and she tells that she cannot live anymore and she died. After this insident she sends a message to patiwada Appalanaidu (soldier) in a dream. She told that search her statue from the west side of Pedda cheruvu(lake) and then she told to build a temple at particular place.
