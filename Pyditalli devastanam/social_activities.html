Annadanam

Sri Sri Sri Pyditallamma Nithya Annadanam Scheme was started by Pyditallamma Trust, Vizianagaram on 23-4-1990 with serving of free food to 500 pilgrims per day at Tirumala by the then Honâ€™ble Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh.

This trust is being run with the donations received from Devotees / Donors from all over the world. The trust deposited all the donations in nationalized banks and the interest accrued on it is being utilized to meet the expenditure of the trust.

Acknowledgement, Income Tax Exemption Certificate under {80(G) 50%} & Donor pass books will be issued to the eligible donors.

Prior to June, 2008, Tokens are being issued to the pilgrims in side Sri Vari Tirumala Temple who had darshan for taking Annaprasadams.
