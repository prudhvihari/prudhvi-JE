Pyditallamma Sirimanotsavam

The main festival that celebrated in Vizianagaram related to pyditallamma is Pyditallamma Sirimanotsavam. This festival is celebrated in large scale in vizianagaram, nearly 10lakhs people from villages around the vizianagaram and from neibhouring districts and towns will come to vizianagaram for the festival.

Siri means goddess Lakshmi(represents wealth) and manu means trunk or log. It is held in September or October of every year after dasara festival every year. this festival is celebrated every year on first tuesday after dasara festival. The priest of the temple, while taking procession between the fort and temple three times in the evening, hangs from the tip of the long, lean wooden staff (measuring 60 feet), raised high into the sky. It is believed that approximately 15 days before Sirimanu utsavam goddess Pyditalli ammavaru would come in the dream of the temple priest and tell him where the sirimanu for that year could be found.
